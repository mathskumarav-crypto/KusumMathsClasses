About Us

Kusum Maths Classes is a dedicated educational initiative launched by Kumar Avinash Sir, an NIT graduate with over 27 years of rich corporate experience. With a strong academic foundation and deep professional exposure, he brings a rare blend of practical insight and conceptual clarity to the field of competitive examination preparation.

Since his graduation, Kumar Avinash Sir has been actively mentoring students, guiding them not only in mathematics but also in building confidence, analytical thinking, and exam temperament. Over the years, he has helped numerous aspirants secure government jobs across various sectors.

Kusum Maths Classes was established with a clear mission — to make quality mathematics education accessible, structured, and result-oriented for students preparing for competitive examinations such as UPSC CSAT, SSC, and other government recruitment exams.

The teaching approach focuses on strong conceptual clarity, practical problem-solving techniques, and extensive practice of previous year questions. The aim is not merely to complete the syllabus, but to ensure that every student develops mastery and confidence in quantitative aptitude.

With decades of professional experience and years of mentoring, Kumar Avinash Sir understands the real challenges faced by aspirants. His guidance combines discipline, strategy, and motivation, helping students stay focused and consistent throughout their preparation journey.

At Kusum Maths Classes, we believe that with the right guidance, disciplined practice, and clear strategy, every determined aspirant can achieve success in government examinations.
